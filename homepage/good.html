Management style

“Do I have a special someone? Well, yeah of course. A bunch of ’em. My employees.”


I often like to consider the folks at Dunder Mifflin as my extended family. My employees love me and I want to be worthy of that kind of regard. I believe it's the bond between us at the Scranton branch that truly makes us the best. I like to be as hands-off as possible, letting my employees have freedom to be themselves. We pride ourselves in being inclusive.
